Video Game Emulation Buddy

An Independant project to build an encyclopedia of emulators that can be used in place of older video game consoles that may be non-functional, beyond repair, or no longer compatible with modern televisions. Specifically, this is meant to help people locate the emulators they need to enjoy specific video games for which they may not remember the name of the console on which it was released

REMEMBER : video game piracy is "illegal", the existence of this document is not supporting the practice of downloading pirated roms, but hopes to preserve the experience of playing older games for the next generation. Support official releases where they are available.
